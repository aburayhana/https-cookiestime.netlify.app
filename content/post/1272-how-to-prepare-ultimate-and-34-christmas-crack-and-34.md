##### Steps to make &#34;Christmas Crack&#34;:

1. Shift any moved saltines. Let cool 5 minutes. Sprinkle the chocolate &amp; butterscotch chips over saltines. Let sit 5 minutes to melt. Take a spatula and spread evenly.
