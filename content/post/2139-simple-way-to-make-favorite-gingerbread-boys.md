##### Instructions to make Gingerbread Boys:

1. Cream shortening &amp; sugar.

1. Cover. Chill for 2-3 hours.
1. Heat oven to 375.
1. Roll dough to 1/4&#34; thick on lightly floured board.
1. Cut with gingerbread boy cutter. Add mini m&amp;ms.
